Jim Forger:

I needed to take a second to let you know that I LOVE LOVE LOVE that you include your entire build process in the project videos rather than just showing final abbreviated results. You can’t imagine how refreshing it was to watch you go back and trim the bottom workbench pieces not once, but twice! In youtube videos it seemed like everybody except me could take a measurement, cut a piece precisely to that measurement, and have the piece fit perfectly every single time. It usually takes me several adjustments to get to the right fit and it’s nice to know I’m not alone. It was also reassuring to watch how many times you checked squaring when assembling the legs . Most youtube videos show a quick check prior to gluing/screwing and their results are always perfect, while I always need to check and recheck multiple times. I hope others find as much value in this as I do so you will continue to show us the “real” behind-the-scene process.
